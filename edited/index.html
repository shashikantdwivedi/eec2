About

Emerging Entrepreneur Award is organized by Career Point University to encourage today's youth towards entrepreneurship.To provide a platform to the students who want to prove their knowledge and skills .

Ideology

To test the feasibility and acceptability of the b-plan in market, as market is dynamic and it keeps on changing so the idea may be acceptable today but it has to be modified on the day when it is to be implemented.

For whom it is for ?

This would benefit the ones who want to be the entrepreneur of tomorrow and the ones who want to showcase their talent/ideas to masses and want it to be assessed by experts.

Details

  • 2nd EMERGING ENTREPRENEUR AWARD’19 will be held in two phases.
  • Registration will be started from 15th june,19.
  • The decision of the jury will be considered as final.
  • Students of Career Point cannot participate in this competition.
  • There will be registration fees of ₹ 300/- for every participant team.
  • The competition will be held at Career Point University, CP tower 2, Kota.
  • Eligibility Criteria
    • Students of class 12th or Graduate students from any field can participate.
    • Students can participate individually or in a team.
    • Maximum 4 participants are allowed in a team.

Structure of the event

  • Phase 1
    • Team will have registered with us for 1st phase.
    • To register, a link will be available on our website from 13th Jun 2019 to 30th Jun 2019.
    • Clicking on the link will open a form that is divided in two parts.
    • In the first part, students have to fill their personal information and in second part, students have to fill information about their innovative business plan.
    • Expert panel will overview the plan and provide suggestions (if required) and Shortlist 7 best teams for participation in II phase.
    • Qualified team will be informed by 08th July through email to participate on final day.
  • Phase 2
    • The shortlisted teams has to be present on time with their detailed Business plan in MS Word or PDF format (Hard copy) and give power point presentation in front of the judge’s panel on Date of final Presentation.
    • Maximum time for presentation will be 15 minutes and number of slides should not exceed 20.
    • Questions will be asked by Expert panel

Evaluation

  • A committee of professionals will shortlist the team on the basis of the Business Plan details provide by them in the first phase of the competition.
  • In the final phase the following points will be considered for assessment:
    • Feasibility of the business plan.
    • Communication skills of participants.
    • Presentation skills.
    • Quality of presentation.
    • Answers given by participants.
